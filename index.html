Our Service

vroom vroom! ==>

Since 2019, car servicing and maintenance has been provided from our workshop in Deal, Kent. Rather than following standard checklists, each vehicle is looked at individually, considering its age, how it's used, and what it actually needs. The goal is a truly bespoke service, with an approach tailored to meet each customer's unique needs. The workshop is well equipped for technical work including: electrical diagnostics, cambelt work and other issues that require specialist attention. Electric vehicles and legacy petrol and diesel cars are both welcome, whatever you've brought, we'll work with it. Getting things right the first time is the priority. Customer satisfaction drives everything; this means as well as servicing your car we provide peace of mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Bespoke Vehicle Maintenance different?
We don't offer mindless services or use generic checklists. Every car and customer receives a unique approach tailored to their specific needs and circumstances. We treat each vehicle individually rather than following a one-size-fits-all approach.
Do you work on electric vehicles?
Yes, we maintain and repair electric vehicles. Our technical expertise extends to modern automotive technology including electric drivetrains and charging systems. 		What types of vehicles do you service?
We work on cars and small vans up to approximately 20 years old, including electric vehicles. We don't service classic cars, motorcycles, large vans, or lorries.
Do you offer MOT testing?
No, we don't provide MOT testing. However, we carry out all other aspects of mechanical repair and maintenance, specialising in technical work like diagnostics and cambelt replacement. 		What areas do you serve?
We're based in Deal, Kent, and serve customers from as far as they're willing to travel. Many clients come from across Kent and surrounding areas for our specialist technical services.
What are your specialities?
We specialise in technical work requiring computer literacy and advanced diagnostics - electrical diagnostics, cambelt replacement, complex engine issues, and modern automotive technology including electric vehicles.
